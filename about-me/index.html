About me
Hi there! My name is Kacper Polarczyk and I am a front-end developer, freelancer & UX/UI designer based in Poland. I use MasterCode as my nickname across social media. I specialize in creating user-friendly, visually appealing, and functional websites. As a Front End Developer and UX/UI designer, I have been working in the industry for over a year. In that time, I have gained a strong understanding of HTML, CSS, and JS, and I am always looking for ways to improve my skills and stay up to date with the latest developments in the field. As a web developer, I am passionate about creating fast, easy-to-use, beautiful, and accessible websites that enhance the user experience. I believe that a well-designed website can make a huge difference in how people interact with a business or organization, and I strive to create websites that are not only functional, but also engaging. Overall, I am committed to making a positive impact on the web, and I am always looking for ways to innovate and improve the user experience for everyone . You can find me on Twitter, Discord, Github, Linkedin.
